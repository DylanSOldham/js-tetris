TETRIS

Up Arrow: Rotate Piece
Down Arrow: Accelerate Descent
Left Arrow: Move Left
Right Arrow: Move Right

Context: Tetris

I've been gradually developing a graphics framework to help me more easily create games and simulations on the HTML canvas. I've been adding functionality to it incrementally by creating small projects with it. This is one of the first of those projects.

The web browser provides a level of convenience for sharing small projects that I don't think any other platform could provide. Almost any application is immediately supported by a wide variety of operating systems - although mobile ones may require special attention to avoid the requirement of a keyboard. I also like being able to show my work to my friends just by sending a direct link, rather than by coordinating a download. It also makes showing my work on a portfolio pretty simple.
