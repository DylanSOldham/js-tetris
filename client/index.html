TETRIS

Up Arrow: Rotate Piece
Down Arrow: Accelerate Descent
Left Arrow: Move Left
Right Arrow: Move Right

Context: Tetris

I had been gradually developing a graphics framework to help me more easily create games and simulations on the HTML canvas. This was one of the first of those projects.

The web browser provides a level of convenience for sharing small projects that I don't think any other platform could provide. Almost any web application is immediately available on all major platforms - although mobile platforms may require special attention. I also like being able to show my work to my friends just by sending a direct link, rather than by coordinating a download. This also makes showing my work on a portfolio pretty simple.
